## 📌 Project Overview
This project is created as part of the Digital Literacy course. As a Student Digital Ambassador, the objective of this project is to promote awareness about digital literacy among students. It covers essential skills such as understanding digital tools, maintaining a professional online presence, communicating effectively, and staying safe from cyber threats.

The project is divided into five tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy.

---

## 📚 Module-wise Summary

### 🔹 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created a one-page infographic using Canva. The infographic explains the concept of digital literacy, safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and email etiquette. It helps students understand how to use digital platforms effectively and responsibly.

---

### 🔹 Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
In this task, I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and HackerRank. These platforms are important for building a professional digital identity. I added my educational details on LinkedIn and created a profile README on GitHub.

---

### 🔹 Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
In this task, I explored HackerRank by solving a beginner-level coding problem. I also created a Google Form titled "Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz" to test students’ knowledge. This task helped me understand coding practice and collaboration tools.

### 🔹 Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Communication
In this task, I wrote two professional emails:
- Request for assignment deadline extension  
- Application for internship opportunity  

I also created a Social Media Do’s and Don’ts checklist to promote responsible online behavior.

---

### 🔹 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
In this task, I created a case study on phishing, explaining how cybercrime occurs and its impact. I also prepared a prevention checklist to help students stay safe online.
