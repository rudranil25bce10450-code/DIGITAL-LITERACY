I used Canva to create a digital literacy infographic. The design includes topics such as safe internet practices, digital tools for students, and email etiquette. The infographic highlights the importance of strong passwords, avoiding phishing links, and maintaining a professional online presence. Canva provided easy templates and design elements, making the task visually appealing. One interesting aspect was selecting the right colors and layout to make the information clear and engaging. However, arranging content in a limited space was slightly challenging. Overall, this task improved my creativity and understanding of digital awareness.
