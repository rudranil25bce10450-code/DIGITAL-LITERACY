
Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (Checklist)
Do’s ✅
Think before posting and ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Don’ts ❌
Do not share personal information like phone number, address, or passwords.
Do’s ✅
Maintain a professional and positive online image.
Don’ts ❌
Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
Do’s ✅
Verify information before sharing to prevent spreading fake news.
Don’ts ❌
Do not believe or forward unverified rumors or misinformation.
Do’s ✅
Respect others’ opinions and engage politely in discussions.
Don’ts ❌
Avoid cyberbullying, trolling, or arguing aggressively online.
Do’s ✅
Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts.
Don’ts ❌

Do not accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious profiles.
